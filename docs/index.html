Static vs dynamic rest-space in the downfolded $T$-matrixTheory2026-06-24CompleteDerivation note. The two-layer $T$-matrix $T=[1-T^R G^A(\omega)]^{-1}T^R$ with rest dressing $T^R=[1-gG^R(\omega_0)]^{-1}g$: shown identical to the Feshbach self-energy $\tilde V=W_{PP}+\Sigma_P$, and the split proven exact iff $T^R$ is taken at the running $\omega$ — so freezing $T^R(\omega_0)$ is the only frequency approximation in Layer 1. Static error $\delta T^R\approx(\omega-\omega_0)T^R(\partial_\omega G^R)T^R$, equivalently a $Z=(1+\beta)^{-1}<1$ renormalization, valid only for $W_A/\Delta\ll1$ (degrades as the active space grows). Pole positions are weakly affected (V$_S$: static $+1.209$ vs dynamic $+1.192$ eV, $Z\approx0.99$); spectral weight and satellites are not. Includes the exact dynamic $T^R(\omega)$ form (cheap: one GEMM/$\omega$) and a comparison plan. Derivation only — not yet run.Open derivation →
Born, self-consistent Born & the self-consistent $T$-matrix (SCTMA)Theory2026-06-24CompleteDerivation of the dilute-defect self-energy hierarchy: 1st Born ($n_d vG_0v$, = golden-rule EDI) → SCBA ($n_d v\bar G v$, rainbow diagrams, no bound states) → $T$-matrix ($n_d v[1-G_0v]^{-1}$, our current beyond-Born result) → SCTMA ($n_d v[1-\mathcal G v]^{-1}$ with $\mathcal G=[(G^A)^{-1}-\Sigma]^{-1}$, self-consistent). Proven to be the $O(n_d)$ limit of CPA; mapped onto the Koster–Slater pipeline (one extra self-consistency loop, $G^A\!\to\!\mathcal G$). Numerically tested on MoS$_2$ V$_S$: at the physical $n_d{=}2.78\%$ SCTMA moves the $a_1$ by only $-9$ meV (does NOT lift it toward DFT $+0.14$); its actual effect is a disorder broadening of the deep $e$ level. The band-edge $a_1$ needs DFT self-consistency.Open derivation →
